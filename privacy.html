Privacy Policy

for pokertable

Last updated: 2025-01-01

Overview

pokertable is a dealer utility for in-person Texas Hold’em. We do not run ads or sell data. We collect only the minimum needed to run a multiplayer session.

Data We Collect

  • Player name you enter — used to label seats in your game.
  • Random device/session ID — generated to keep players in sync.

How We Use Data

Exclusively to provide app functionality (hosting/joining games, dealing cards, board display). No tracking or advertising.

Sharing

No sale of personal data. Our backend provider (Supabase) processes data only to operate the service.

Retention

Session data is short-lived and may be pruned automatically. Player names are stored only with session records.

Security

All communication uses HTTPS/TLS.

Your Choices

You may request deletion of session data that can be reasonably associated with you. Contact us.

Children

Intended for users 13+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
