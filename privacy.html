Privacy Policy

for pokertable

Last updated: 2025-01-01

Overview

pokertable is a dealer utility for in‑person Texas Hold’em. We do not run ads or sell data. The app collects only the minimum information needed to operate a multiplayer session.

Data We Collect

  • Player name you enter — used to label seats in your game.
  • Random device/session ID — generated on your device to keep players in sync.

How We Use Data

We use this information solely to provide core app functionality (hosting and joining games, dealing cards, and showing the board). We do not use your data for tracking or advertising.

Sharing

No sale of personal data. Data is stored/processed using our backend provider (Supabase) only to operate the service.

Retention

Game sessions and associated temporary IDs are short‑lived and may be pruned automatically. Player names are stored only with their session records.

Security

All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS. We follow reasonable measures to protect data in transit and at rest.

Your Choices

You may ask us to delete session data that can be reasonably associated with you. Contact us using the email below.

Children

pokertable is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
